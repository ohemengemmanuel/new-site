About Us

Emperial Waste is dedicated to delivering reliable waste management and facility services across Kasoa, Weija, and beyond.

We specialize in:

  • Providing large-scale waste disposal solutions.
  • Supplying skip containers and rubbish bins for businesses and communities.
  • Offering disinfection and fumigation services to promote healthier environments.

Our mission is to create cleaner, safer, and more sustainable surroundings while ensuring professional service delivery to all our clients.
